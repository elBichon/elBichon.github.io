ABOUT THE SOLUTION

Key features of our product

Our offer

  • As a teacher or a facilitator, the way you convey your message is as important if not more important than the carried message itself.
    To make sure that you are being understood, you must correlate your posture and non verbal communication to your speech. You should also adapt to the reaction of your audience to make sure to reach your teaching goals.

  • Using computer vision, data extraction and data aggregation, the solution extracts features not only from your audience (gender, age bucket, diplayed emotion...) but also from the speaker, speech to text, tone, posture, emotion... and correlate them in relevant visualizations providing real time insights.

  • This solution allows you to improve your presentation skills by providing real time audience feedback but also providing post presentation analysis and enabling you to make the impact you want.
